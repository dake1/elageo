Land Registration

Obtaining proper title documents to a property is a very frustrating and time-consuming experience for most people. We firmly believe that land registration is a cornerstone in today’s economy which enables everyone who deals with property to do so with confidence. Consultancy covering a wide range of disciplines connected with land administration will be at your services including (a) Property demarcation and description. (b) Liaising with solicitors on engrossment. (c) Stamping procedures at the Land Valuation Divisions of the Lands Commission (d) Registration/Title Perfection at the Public and Vested Land and Management Division and the Land Registration Division of the Lands Commission. (e) Obtaining Town Planning consent and approval. F ) we facilitate the preparation of Deeds for conveyance for clients on request.
